## Intent

The intent of this process is to provide me the ability to 

* Track my Fidelity portfolio by asset classes vs Target Percentages

* To provide a flask UI able to visualize the asset classes data and metrics enabling me to analyze opportunities

* To Download Data sufficient for our signals engine on a daily basis
	Phase 1: For small Universe
	Phase 2: For extended Universe

* To programatically and using latest data science analyze the data for signals

Asset Classes Should Include  (equity sectors, bonds, commodities, forex, crypto) using end-of-day price/volume data. Pairs quantitative signals with the operator's macro judgment to generate rebalancing guidance — with deliberate emphasis on sell signals, which are behaviorally harder to act on. Runs locally on free data (yfinance + FRED). No auto-trading. Operator decides.
